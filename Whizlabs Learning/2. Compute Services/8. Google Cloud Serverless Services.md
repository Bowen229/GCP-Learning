### 1. **Cloud Functions**
- **Description**: Run single-purpose functions in response to events.

### 3. **App Engine**

- **Use Case**: Scalable SaaS apps, mobile backends, websites

### 4. **Workflows**

- **Use Case**: Data pipelines, batch job orchestration, business process flows
